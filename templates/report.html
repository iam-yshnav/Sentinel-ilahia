Quick Guide to Threat Submission

  • Title: Give a clear, concise threat title.
  • Summary: Briefly explain the threat's nature.
  • IOCs: List key indicators (IPs, domains, etc.).
  • Platforms: Specify impacted systems or apps.
  • Details: Provide an in-depth description.
  • Severity: Rate the threat's impact.
  • Attach Evidence: Optional, but helpful for analysis.
