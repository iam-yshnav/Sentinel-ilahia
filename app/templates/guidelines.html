Guidelines

Welcome to SENTINEL. Please follow these guidelines:

  • Ensure that the submitted threats are real and verifiable.
  • Provide a detailed description of the threat, including potential impacts.
  • Avoid submitting duplicate threats.
  • Companies must list accurate server details for precise threat detection.
  • Users submitting verified threats will receive appropriate rewards.
  • Respect privacy and security regulations when submitting reports.

For any questions, contact our support team.
