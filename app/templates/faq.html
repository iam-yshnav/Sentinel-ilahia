Frequently Asked Questions

What is this platform about?

This platform allows users to submit cybersecurity threats and helps companies identify which servers are affected.

How do I submit a threat?

You can submit a threat by filling out the threat submission form available on our website.

How are threats verified?

Our team reviews and verifies each submission before adding it to the system.

What rewards do I get for submitting a valid threat?

Users who submit verified threats will receive rewards based on the severity and accuracy of the submission.

How can companies list their servers?

Companies can register and add their server details through the company portal on our website.

Is there a limit to the number of threats I can submit?

No, you can submit as many threats as you find, but ensure they are valid and not duplicates.

What happens if I submit false information?

Submitting false information can lead to penalties, including being banned from the platform.

How can I track the status of my submitted threat?

You can track the status of your submission through your user dashboard.

Can I edit a threat submission after submitting it?

No, once submitted, a threat cannot be edited. If necessary, you can submit an updated version.
